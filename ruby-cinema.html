No.01 Dessert Cocktail

Ruby Cinema

Ruby Cinema is a layered dessert cocktail with raspberry, pomegranate, crème de cacao, vodka, and rose-scented cream.

Fruity brightness blends into chocolate and cream, like a drinkable red movie.

Ruby Cinema Recipe

Raspberry & Pomegranate (base layer)

Crème de Cacao 20 ml

Vodka 30 ml

Rose-scented cream float (top)

Layer carefully / Serve in goblet / Garnish with chocolate
